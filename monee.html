Monee banking app

Research the key need

A UI/UX designer's task is to create a mobile app for target customers who need their first banking services. The app should be user-friendly and visually appealing, and convey our client's values.

Tailor the solution

The app should provide a global view of accounts and cards, current balance, and monthly expenses, offer bank products directly or indirectly.

The User Interface of the solution needed to feature a color palette and the typography provided by the brand team.

Solution

UI Decisions

  • I believed that incorporating gradients of two base colors will help Monee stand out from the competition and be more approachable to the Target Audience.
  • Adding contextualized advice to transaction notifications in the feed should make recommended bank products hard to overlook and make user experienced more personalized.
  • Increased focus on sharing expenses (sending/requesting funds and collaborating on the goals) should meet the needs of the Target Audience.
