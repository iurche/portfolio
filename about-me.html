Having strong knowledge and hands-on experience in UX, currently I'm actively learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I strive to develop products that take into account the user experience, business goals, and technical feasibility.

As a UX writer, I understand the power of words to shape the user experience. I understand the difference between Voice and Tone, and know why "Oopsie!" rarely, if ever, works. I aim to write copy that is clear, concise, and engaging so that users can easily understand the benefits of the product.
