About me

Background

I'm a Senior Product Designer with 6+ years of experience in eCommerce and SaaS. I've worked across the full product lifecycle — from early-stage discovery to shipped features — most recently at VistaPrint, designing self-service and support experiences that measurably reduce contact volume and improve customer outcomes. Currently completing an MSc in Product Management (June 2026).

How I work

I run discovery independently, frame problems before jumping to solutions, and use qualitative and quantitative research to inform direction. I'm comfortable presenting to stakeholders, facilitating alignment, and making design decisions with data — not just intuition. I treat design as a continuous process, not a handoff.

What I bring

An early adopter of AI tools — I use AI to bridge design and implementation, reducing the gap between design intent and built output. I work best where outcomes matter more than outputs. Accessibility-first thinking (WCAG 2.2), UX copy written as part of the design process, and HTML & CSS prototypes when fidelity matters.
